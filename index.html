Tasty Recipe Ideas from Pipkin
 

Chicken Stir Fry

Ingredients
2 tbsp Pipkin Coconut Oil, 450g Boneless Chicken, 2 Garlic Cloves, 1 Onion, 350g Stir Fry, Vegetables, 3 tbsp Soy Sauce, ½ inch Ginger, 1 tbsp sweet and sour, Rice or Noodles to serve.
Instructions
1. Chop and slice the onion. Crush the garlic and peel/grate the ginger. Slice chicken into thin strips.
2. Heat half the oil in a wok. Add the chicken and stir-fry for 4-5 minutes until lightly coloured. Remove from the pan and transfer to a plate. Heat the remaining coconut oil in the pan and add onion, ginger and garlic. Stir-fry for 30 seconds or until softened and add the vegetables.
3. Return the chicken to the pan and adding the soy and sauce. Stir until evenly mixed, then cover and cook for a further 2 minutes until the chicken is tender and the veggies are slightly softened.

Butternut Squash Soup

Ingredients
2 tbsp Pipkin Coconut Oil, 1 Butternut Squash, 1 Onion, 1 Garlic Clove, 3 Rosemary Sprigs, 600ml, Chicken/Vegetable Stock, 150ml White Wine, 100ml Double Cream.
Instructions
Peel and chop the butternut squash, onion and garlic and place into a roasting tin. Add half the coconut oil and rosemary. Roast for 30 mins, turning squash with a slotted spoon halfway through. Check the squash, onion and garlic is golden and soft.
2. Remove rosemary and transfer contents to a food processor adding stock, wine and double cream. Add seasoning to taste and whizz up.
3. Transfer mixture to a pan, heat up and eat.
